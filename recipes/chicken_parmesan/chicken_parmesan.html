Chicken Parmesan

Description

Traditional chicken parm using a cheese spread riccota and fortified with sharp cheddar

Ingredients

  • 2 skinless, boneless chicken breasts
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 pinch back pepper
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 3/4 cup dry bread crumbs
  • 1/2 cup light olive oil for frying
  • 1/2 cup riccota cheese
  • 1/2 cup cheddar cheese
  • 2 tablespoons grated parm cheese
  • 1 cup marina sauce
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh flat-leaf parsley

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven 500deeg F, line a rimmed baking sheet with aluminum foil
  2. Season chicken with salt and pepper, coat with flour
  3. Pour beaten egg over the chicken , transfer to bread crumbs and coat each side
  4. Heat 1/2 inch olive oil over medium heat. Cook the chiken until crispy and golden, transfer to bakingg sheet
  5. Mix the cheese in a bowl, stir in salt, pepper cayanne and olive oil. Spread over the breasts
  6. Bake on center rack until cheese is meltedfor 10 - 12 mins
  7. To serve ladle the heated mariana sauce on warm plates, place chicken in center with chopped parsley
