Chiles Rellenos

Description

Authentic chille relleno recipe

Ingredients

  • Anaheim chile pepper
  • White mexican cheese
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 3/4 flour
  • 1 cup vegetable oil

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven
  2. Place the peppers onto the baking and broil until skins are blackend
  3. Remove the skin from peppers, then cut a slit down the long side and remove the seeds
  4. Stuff peppers with strips of cheese
  5. Whisk egg yolks and baking powder in a bowl until combined
  6. Beat the egg whites whit a mixer until stiff peaks form
  7. Fold the whites into the yolk mixture
  8. Roll each pepper into flour, then dip into the egg mixture and fry the peppers unit lightly golden and the cheese has melted
