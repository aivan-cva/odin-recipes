Cheesy Ham

Description

An easy ham, cheese, and potato casserole.

Ingredients

  • 1 package of hash brown potatoes.
  • 1 package ham steak didec
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 onion
  • 1 can condensed cream
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • salt and black pepper

Instructions

Step 1

Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F

Step 2

Stir potatoes, ham, cheese, onion, cream and butter in a large casserole; season with salt and pepper.

Step 3

Cover with aluminum foil, bake for 45 minutes to 1 hour until the cheese melts.
