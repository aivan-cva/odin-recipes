Eggs Benedict

Description

Poached eggs on top of an english muffin and bacon, with hollandaise sauce on top

Ingredients

For the eggs
  • 3 table spoons of white vinegar
  • 3 eggs
  • 2 english muffins
  • bacon
For the sauce
  • 125g butter
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 1/2 table spoon white vinegar
  • lemon juice

Steps

Poached eggs
  1. Bring a deep pan of water to the boil and add 3 tbsp white vinegar at low heat.
  2. Break each egg into a different cup. Prepare a paper towel to dry the eggs.
  3. Swirl the simmering water creating a vortex, carefully add one egg at a time for a minute.
  4. Very carefully remove the eggs from the water and dry them.
Hollandaise Sauce
  1. Use a glass bowl on top of the pan with water.
  2. Melt the butter in a sauce pan skimming any solids from the surface.
  3. Add 2 egg yolks, 1/2 tbsp of white vinegar, the juice of one lemon a pinch of salt and pepper.
  4. Incorporate everything
Eggs Benedict
  1. Put the muffin with bacon.
  2. Put one poached egg and cover it with one spoon of hollandaise sauce.
