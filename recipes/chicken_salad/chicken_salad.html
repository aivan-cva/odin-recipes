Eggs Benedict

Description

High protein dish. Marinated chicken breast with satay sauce.

Ingredients

For the salad
  • 1 table spoon tamari
  • 1 tablespoon curry powder
  • 1 spoon cinnamon
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1 spoon honey
  • 2 skinless chicken breast fillets
  • 1 tablespoon sweet chill sauce
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • 1 cucumber
  • seeds

Steps

Salad
  1. Pour the tamari into a large dish and stir in the curry powder, garlic and honey. Mix well
  2. Slice the chicken breasts and mit them in the sauce make sure to coat them.
  3. Mix chill sauce, lime juice, and 1 spoon of water
  4. Cook the chicken in a large pan with olive oil for 5 minutes, set aside covered
  5. While the chicken rests toss the lettuce and the cucumbers and seeds onto a plate, spoon over a little sauce. SLice the chicken on top of the salad and spoon over the remaining sauce
